Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What is this app for?

    This app is designed to screen individuals for Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) based on behavioral questions.

  • Is this quiz a medical diagnosis?

    No, the quiz is not a diagnostic tool. It provides a preliminary screening, but a professional evaluation is required for a diagnosis.

  • How are the results calculated?

    The results are based on a machine learning model that predicts the likelihood of ASD based on your responses.
